CBRS Deployment Updates

Status of CBRS hardware installations, configurations, and experiments across the 3.55–3.7 GHz Citizens Broadband Radio Service band.

Deployment Summary

The CBRS deployment consisting of Celona AP 20 deployed indoors and AP 21 deployed outdoors. The AP-20 has the flexibility to be configured on either 4G with up to two 20 MHz aggregated channels or a single 20/40 MHz 5G channel, while the AP-21 can only operate with a single 20/40 MHz 5G channel.
